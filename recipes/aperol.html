Aperol Sour

Chill your glass in the freezer while preparing the cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces Aperol
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces gin
  • 1 fluid ounce fresh lemon juice
  • 1 fluid ounce simple syrup
  • 1 large egg white
  • 2 dashes bitters, such as Angostura BittersÂ®
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Combine Aperol, gin, lemon juice, simple syrup, egg white, and bitters in a cocktail shaker.
  2. Shake mixture until frothy, 30 to 45 seconds.
  3. Add ice to shaker, and shake again until chilled, 15 to 30 seconds more.
  4. Strain into chilled coupe.
  5. Garnish with an additional dash of bitters, if desired.
